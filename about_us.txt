About Us
==============================

The swimwear brand Shellysunwear (currently based in the US) has its origins in the city of Caracas in 19XX, and has been promoted since then by its creator and founder Marisela Barazarte.

We are a leading clothing company with 30 years of experience  in the manufacturing and marketing of Swimwears,apparels and beach accesories. Thanks to this experience, we can offer you the best personalized attention, the best tailored swimwear service, at any time you require, we guarantee your satisfaction and happiness when purchasing any of our products.
